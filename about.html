About House of Quazi

Company Information

House of Quazi is a pioneering artisan company established in 2010, focusing on unique handcrafted products inspired by traditional techniques and modern aesthetics. Our goal is to create sustainable, high-quality items that tell a story and connect consumers with the craftsmen behind them.

Background Story

Founded by Quazi in the heart of Bangladesh, House of Quazi began with a vision to empower local artisans through fair trade practices and to preserve cultural heritage. Over the years, we have expanded our reach globally, sharing the beauty and craftsmanship of our handmade pieces with a wider audience. Each item we produce is a reflection of our commitment to quality and sustainability, providing a unique touch to every home.
